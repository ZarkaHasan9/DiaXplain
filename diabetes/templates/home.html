Healthy Lifestyle Guidelines for Diabetes

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fiber, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Engage in regular physical activity, at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly as advised by your doctor.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid sugary beverages.
  • Get adequate sleep and manage stress through meditation or hobbies.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
  • Schedule regular check-ups to monitor and manage your condition.
