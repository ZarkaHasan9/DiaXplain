About DiaXplain

DiaXplain is an intelligent, user-friendly platform designed to help you assess and understand your risk of diabetes. Powered by machine learning, diaXplain not only provides early risk prediction but also offers clear, interpretable insights and lifestyle guidance to support better health decisions. Our goal is to make preventive health accessible, explainable, and personalized for every individual.
